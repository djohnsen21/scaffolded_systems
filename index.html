Welcome to Scaffolded System

Empowering Changemakers Through Structure

This tool helps individuals and small teams turn big ideas into clear, fundable, and actionable plans using a “Theory of Change” framework.

Rather than starting with tasks or to-do lists, this system encourages you to define the outcome you want to achieve, and envision the real-world change you desire.

From there, you’ll map out a sequence of non-action-oriented preconditions — specific conditions that must be true for your goal to become possible.

These preconditions are not actions (e.g., “host an event”), but rather states of the world or community (e.g., “community members are aware of the issue”). By focusing on what must exist, rather than what must be done, this tool helps you clarify your vision and think more strategically about change.

Ready to Make a Change?

This tool helps you plan and map your project in a structured way.

What We Need From You:

  • End Goal: The outcome you want to achieve.
  • Preconditions: Non-action conditions necessary for your goal.
